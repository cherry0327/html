We have created a number of features to make your shopping experience as easy as possible. For example:

  • Import Favourites – already shop online with another supermarket? You can import your favourites from any retailer instantly so you can save time as well as money.
  • Quick Shop – next to the search bar is the quick shop button where you can copy and paste your weekly shopping list. Type your list into the Quick Shop notepad and we’ll fetch all the options on the items you need in an instant.
  • Savvy Shop – looking for a bargain? The Savvy Shop shelf are the ‘real deals’ which are at least 30% less than their average price
  • Swap & Save – We’ll let you know when you can swap an item to a cheaper alternative or save more by choosing a product with a better unit price.
